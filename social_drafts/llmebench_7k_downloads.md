**What is LLMeBench?**
LLMeBench (LLM Effectiveness Benchmarking) is an open-source, **plug-and-play framework** for systematically evaluating Large Language Models on standard NLP tasks. It was built to make LLM benchmarking **simple, reproducible, and accessible** to researchers, developers, and practitioners — whether you're comparing models, integrating new tasks, or running large-scale evaluations across languages.

✨ **Highlights:**
🔹 Plug-and-play evaluation across many NLP tasks
🔹 Multilingual & cross-lingual benchmarking out of the box
🔹 Easy integration of new models, datasets, and tasks
🔹 Reproducible experiment workflows for the research community

A huge thank you to everyone who has used, cited, contributed to, or shared LLMeBench. Every download, issue, and PR pushes the project forward. 🚀
