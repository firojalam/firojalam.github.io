**CritiSense** is a mobile-first critical digital literacy app developed by QCRI at HBKU. It is designed to strengthen resilience against fake news, propaganda, and online manipulation through interactive learning, quizzes, prebunking, and AI-assisted content analysis.

The platform combines structured micro-lessons with hands-on practice so users can learn how to recognize manipulation tactics across text, images, and online content. It aims to make media-literacy training practical, accessible, and engaging for a broad multilingual audience.
