**Short Bio:** I am a **Senior Scientist** at the [Qatar Computing Research Institute](https://www.hbku.edu.qa/en/qcri). I received my PhD in Computer Science from the [University of Trento](https://www.unitn.it/en), under the supervision of [Prof. Giuseppe Riccardi](https://www.disi.unitn.it/riccardi), where I also earned my master's degree. I completed my undergraduate studies at BRAC University. I have authored numerous peer-reviewed publications and served as LPI and PI on several local, international, and EU-funded research projects. Beyond my research, I actively organize workshops and shared tasks, and mentor students and junior researchers. My work focuses on advancing AI technologies for low-resource and underrepresented languages.

## Research Interests:
- **AI / Deep Learning / Machine Learning:** Large Language Models, Multitask and Multimodal Learning, Multilingual Models, Multilingual and Multimodal Representation Learning

- **Applied Contexts:** Social Media Content Analysis, Human–LLM Interaction
